About Me
I am currently employed at Arizona State University and am also a nondegree graduate student at the Tempe campus. My academic background is in mathematics, with a focus on applied and numerical methods.
My interests include numerical linear algebra, data analysis, and algorithmic methods for working with real-world datasets. On this site, I document projects, share code and datasets, and maintain a record of my academic and technical work.
This website serves as a professional portfolio for employers and collaborators who want to see examples of my work, the tools I use, and the types of problems I am interested in solving.
