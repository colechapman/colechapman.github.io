Selected projects in applied mathematics, numerical methods, and data analysis.
Nonnegative Matrix Factorization (NMF) for Dimensionality Reduction
Implemented and compared update rules for NMF as a dimensionality reduction / low-rank factorization method. Starting from a full (nonnegative) data matrix, the project constructs a lower-dimensional representation via matrix factors and evaluates convergence behavior and reconstruction quality across: HALS, FAST-HALS, and multiplicative updates (ALS-style).

Completed and documented solutions to Vasilik’s SQL practice set. Includes query design, joins, grouping/aggregation, window functions where applicable, and clear formatting so results are easy to verify.
